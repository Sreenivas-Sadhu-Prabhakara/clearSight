Core Framework: Theory of Constraints

The Constraint: Surgeon/OR Availability.

The Strategy: We cannot buy more time, so we must stop wasting it. Every minute the OR is idle is lost throughput that can never be recovered.

  • Step 1 (Identify): Diagnostic audit reveals 45 min avg time vs 32 min best practice.
  • Step 2 (Exploit): Standardize "Best Performer" techniques (32 min).
  • Step 3 (Subordinate): Pre-staging of patients so OR never waits.
  • Step 4 (Elevate): Expand OR hours only after efficiency is maximized.
