Business Overview

ClearSight Cataracts is a specialized ophthalmology center focused on cataract surgery. The organization operates a single high-volume surgical center facing meaningful operational challenges.

  • Mission: Provide high-quality, efficient cataract surgery services to the local community.
  • Market Position: Faces competition from larger hospitals and other specialized clinics. Patient throughput and cost efficiency are critical.
  • Clinical Excellence: Staffed by Dr. Connors (CMO) and a team of surgeons, but struggling with operational bottlenecks.

End-to-End Operational Flow

The patient journey from intake to surgery. The Operating Room (OR) is the primary constraint.

Organizational Structure

Key Departments:

  • Clinical Team: Surgeons (Dr. Connors + others), Nurses, Anesthesiologists.
  • Administrative: Scheduling, Billing, Reception.
  • Management: Medical Director (Dr. Connors), Clinic Administrator.

Competitive Landscape

ClearSight is struggling with throughput. While clinical outcomes are good, the financial performance is lagging due to inability to process more patients despite having demand and physical capacity. Competitors include streamlined high-volume centers that have optimized their flows.

Strategic Insight: ClearSight operates in a mature market with commoditized services. Competitive differentiation requires operational excellence: faster access to surgery, superior outcomes, and best-in-class patient experience. Current underutilization of capacity (65% vs. 95% potential) represents both a challenge and an opportunity.
