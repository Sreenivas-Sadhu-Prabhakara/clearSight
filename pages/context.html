Business Overview

ClearSight Cataracts is a specialized ophthalmology center focused on cataract surgery. The organization operates a single high-volume surgical center facing meaningful operational challenges.

  • Mission: Provide high-quality, efficient cataract surgery services to the local community.
  • Market Position: Competition from larger hospitals and specialized clinics. Throughput and efficiency are critical.
  • Clinical Excellence: Dr. Connors (CMO) leads the surgical team, but operational bottlenecks limit growth.

End-to-End Operational Flow

The patient journey from intake to post-op. The OR is the primary constraint.

Organizational Structure

  • Clinical Team: Surgeons (Dr. Connors + others), Nurses, Anesthesiologists.
  • Administrative: Scheduling, Billing, Reception.
  • Management: Medical Director (Dr. Connors), Clinic Administrator.

Strategic Insight

ClearSight operates in a mature market with commoditized services. Competitive differentiation requires operational excellence: faster access to surgery, superior outcomes, and best-in-class patient experience. Current underutilization of capacity (50% vs. 85-90% potential) represents both a challenge and an opportunity.
