Business Overview

ClearEyes Cataracts is a specialized ophthalmology center focused on cataract surgery. The organization operates a single high-volume surgical center facing meaningful operational challenges‚Äîprimarily around patient yield and conversion rather than surgical capacity.

  • Mission: Provide high-quality, efficient cataract surgery services to the local community.
  • Market Position: Competition from larger hospitals and specialized clinics. Patient retention and conversion are critical.
  • Operational Challenge: Insurance authorization delays (~42 days) increase patient no-shows and conversion failures, reducing surgical volume.

The patient journey from intake to post-op. Insurance authorization delays (~42 days) are the primary constraint, causing upstream yield loss through no-shows and conversion failures.

Strategic Insight

ClearEyes operates in a mature market with commoditized services. The root cause of lost surgical volume is not OR inefficiency‚Äîit is upstream yield loss. Surgery capacity is often underutilized due to upstream no-shows rather than physical room limitations.

The 42-day insurance authorization wait creates a critical gap between patient commitment and surgery. During this period, patients disengage, no-show, or seek care elsewhere. Addressing this constraint requires reducing authorization lead times, improving patient engagement during the wait, and optimizing conversion rates‚Äînot just speeding up surgeries.
