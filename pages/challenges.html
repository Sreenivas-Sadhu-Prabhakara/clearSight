Dr. Connors' Assessment

"ClearSight has excellent clinical talent and infrastructure, but we're leaving substantial revenue on the table due to operational inefficiencies. We're operating at 50% capacity (3,000 surgeries) when we could be at 6,000. The combination of surgical delays, pre-op bottlenecks, and workforce instability creates a constrained system where our theoretical maximum throughput far exceeds our actual output."

"We need to apply systematic problem-solving methodology to identify and break these constraints. The question is: what is the single most limiting factor preventing us from achieving our full potential? And how do we subordinate all other decisions to removing that constraint?"
