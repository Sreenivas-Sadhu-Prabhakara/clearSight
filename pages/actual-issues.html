The system constraint is unreliable patient flow into surgery, not OR capacity. Multiple upstream factors combine to create yield loss through patient no-shows and conversion failures.

This is multi-factor flow instability, not a classic single-resource bottleneck. Unreliable patient flow results from a combination of financial, logistical, emotional, communication, and administrative barriers that cause patients to drop off before surgery.

How Multiple Barriers Create Unreliable Patient Flow

Key Finding: This is multi-factor flow instability, not a classic single-resource bottleneck. The clinic loses potential surgical volume due to a combination of patient-side barriers (financial insecurity, transportation, fear) and operational barriers (PSR communication variability, insurance authorization delays). Addressing any single factor in isolation will yield limited results‚Äîa systems approach targeting multiple root causes simultaneously is required.
