Understanding clearSight: Context & Business Model

A comprehensive analysis of clearSight's cataract surgery operations through systems thinking and process engineering

1. What is clearSight?

clearSight is a specialized eye care facility in India providing cataract surgeries and post-operative care to patients from lower-income segments. The facility operates with a mission to deliver affordable quality cataract surgery, positioning itself as a cost-effective alternative to premium eye hospitals while maintaining good surgical outcomes.

Key Facts:

  • Specializes in high-volume, low-cost cataract surgeries
  • Serves lower-income and middle-income populations
  • Operating in a competitive healthcare market
  • Limited infrastructure and capital constraints
  • Focused on surgical excellence with cost efficiency
